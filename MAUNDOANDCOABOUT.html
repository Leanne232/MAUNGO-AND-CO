ABOUT



At MAUNGO AND CO, strawberries aren’t just fruit — they’re our passion, our craft, and our signature touch of luxury from nature.
Grown under golden sun and tender care, each berry is a jewel bursting with sweetness, handpicked at the peak of perfection.
We believe enjoying strawberries should feel like a little celebration. That’s why our farm blends tradition with elegance, turning
simple harvests into timeless indulgence. From fresh baskets to exquisite creations, our strawberries bring a taste of richness to every
table, every gathering, and every moment worth savoring. More than a farm, we are storytellers of flavor — inviting you to experience strawberries as they were meant to be:
vibrant, joyful, and beautifully refined.



Our Community

We are more than a farm; we are a gathering place for families, food lovers, and dreamers. From sunlit field visits to sharing recipes
and celebrations, our strawberries bring people together. Each berry carries with it the spirit of connection — sweet, memorable,
and timeless. We warmly invite you to taste the difference. Whether enjoyed fresh from the punnet,
crafted into a delicate dessert, or shared at your most
special occasions, our strawberries turn everyday moments into elegant indulgences.

Because here at MAUNGO AND CO, strawberries aren’t just grown — they’re perfected.
